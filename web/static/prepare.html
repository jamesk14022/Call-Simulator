Prepare for your simulation

You are about to have an opportunity to deal with a simulated call. The customer will be simulated as an AI generated character. Act as you would on a real call, listen carefully to the customer's request, and speak clearly.

  • Ensure your speakers are turned on.
  • Ensure you approve any request for microphone access
  • As on a real call, you can hang up, hold or, if appropriate, transfer the caller.

You will be evaluated on the following attributes:

  1. Whether you resolve the call
  2. Handling time
  3. Polite demeanor
  4. If you confirm the customer's intention
  5. If you confirmed whether the customer has any further questions
